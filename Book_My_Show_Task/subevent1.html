0 - 100 Website Creation and Entrepreneurship with E-Commerce

    Buy Tickets Venue Details T & C

This One Day, Complete Hands-On, No Coding workshop will help you create a professional, dynamic and responsive E - Commerce website from zero knowledge. No prior programming or coding knowledge needed to attend this Workshop. Non-technical people with basic computer knowledge also can attend this workshop.

Outcome of this Training:

1. End of the day you can walk out with your own E-commerce and Payment Gateway enabled website.

2. After this workshop, you will be able to start your own small web development venture from the comfort of your home, without any major investment.

3. You will be able to sell your products online using your website.

4. You will be able to advertise your online business using Digital Marketing techniques.

Timings:

9 am to 6 pm [8 hours of Practical Training, 30 minutes of theory]

Topics Covered:

Essential Things

- Introduction to exciting world of Web Development

- Registering a website name (domain name)

- Managing domain renewals

- Procuring server space (web hosting)
